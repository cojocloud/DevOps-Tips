# 🚀 DevOps Tips, Tricks & Engineering Notes

Welcome to **DevOps Tips, Tricks & Engineering Notes** — a curated collection of practical DevOps knowledge, commands, patterns, and real-world solutions gathered from hands-on experience building and operating modern infrastructure.

This repository is designed to serve as a **quick reference guide for engineers, students, and DevOps practitioners** who want practical solutions to everyday problems in cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, automation, and system reliability.
